Spam Fried Rice

By (Username Goes Here)

Folders: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 can Spam, diced
  • 1 cup frozen veggies
  • Soy sauce - to taste
  • Ginger - to taste
  • Salt & pepper - to taste
  • (Optional) Roasted sesame seeds
  • (Optional) Freshly sliced green onion

Steps

  1. In a large pan, toss diced Spam cubes with soy sauce until edges become crispy - about 5 minutes. Set aside.
  2. Scramble eggs and cook in same pan.
  3. Add Spam and frozen veggies to eggs and toss until well-incorporated and veggies are thawed.
  4. Add rice into pan and toss until well-incorporated, seasoning to taste with soy sauce, ginger, and salt and pepper
  5. Top with roasted sesame seeds and a handful of freshly sliced green onions, and enjoy!
